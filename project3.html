Photo Alphabet

Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: In this project, we needed to go find pictures or take pictures of things that resemble the alphabet. In my case, I walked around the campus of San Jose State and took pictures of things that resembled letters. Some of them were in angles, and others were straight forward.
