New Species

Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: In this project, we needed to find pictures on the internet, or use the puictures provided in class to create a new species of animals. I chose a porcupine, a lion, a zebra, and a betta fish. The background was originally more blue, so I got a sunrise background and color graded some of the other things to blend well together.
